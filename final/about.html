About Me

Madi Ford is currently in her 3rd year as an apprentice at Blaque Owl. Growing up in Great Falls, MT, Madi's passion for art started when she was just a little girl. She continued following that passion throughout her life and attended Montana State University to pursue an art degree. Madi Ford tattoos at the shop full time and specializes in American Traditional tattoos.
